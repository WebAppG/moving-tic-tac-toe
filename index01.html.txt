Gameplay & Setup
You'll control pieces on a 3x3 grid, aiming to align them while blocking your opponent. The game includes several key features to customize your experience.

Movement & Turns: Click or tap a piece you own to see valid moves (shown as black dots). Then click a highlighted spot to move. Players alternate turns.

Game Modes: Use the toggles to switch between two-player and single-player (vs. computer) modes. You can also set who moves first and enable a series mode to track wins across multiple games.

Customization & Tracking: Before starting, choose unique colors for each player's pieces from the palette. The scoreboard tracks wins for both players, and the series record resets when you change the game mode.
